Goat, Meat, Cameroon Pepper, Pepper Soup Spice, Scent Leaves/ Basil, Salt, Bouillon Cubes.

Goat meat pepper soup is a staple in most Nigerian households. You can whip up a big batch anytime for a light, satisfying meal. It is also very popular during parties and gatherings.

  • You can get goat meat at the butcher or Asian, Hispanic, or African stores.
  • You can also ask them to cut it into pieces for you to save some time.
  • Wash and drain the goat meat. Cut the onions and set aside.
  • Place the goat meat in a pot and add about 6 cups of water. Add the chopped onions, thyme, bouillon, and salt.
  • Boil the meat till it is tender. It will take roughly an hour. Keep an eye on it and make sure all the water does not evaporate and add a bit more as needed.
  • After about an hour, the water will be well reduced at this point, so add 4 more cups of water
  • Add the pepper soup spice, cayenne pepper, and crayfish.
  • Taste for salt and add more if needed.
  • Simmer for 20 minutes on medium heat.
